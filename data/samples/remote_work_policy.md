# Acme Corp Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probation period are
eligible to work remotely. Contractors and interns must discuss remote
arrangements with their manager.

## Work Hours

Employees are expected to be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to
4:00 PM in their local time zone. Outside of core hours, employees may
structure their day as needed, provided they complete 40 hours per week.

## Equipment

The company provides a laptop, external monitor, keyboard, and mouse for
remote setups. Employees may expense up to $500 per year for additional
home-office equipment with manager approval.

## Communication

All remote employees must:
- Respond to Slack messages within 2 hours during core hours
- Attend daily team standups via video
- Keep their calendar up to date

## Travel Reimbursement

Remote employees visiting the office quarterly may expense travel costs up to
$1,200 per trip, including flights, lodging, and ground transport. Meal per
diems are $75 per day.
